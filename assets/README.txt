Place your gameplay background video here as: gameplay.mp4

This is used by Mode 1 (Reddit Story) and Mode 2 (Did You Know).

Free sources (download the highest resolution portrait/vertical version):
  - Minecraft parkour: https://pixabay.com/videos/search/minecraft/
  - Subway Surfers:    https://pixabay.com/videos/search/subway%20surfers/
  - Satisfying loops:  https://pixabay.com/videos/search/satisfying/

Tips:
  - Pick a vertical (9:16) video if possible, or the pipeline will crop it
  - 60-120 seconds of footage is plenty — it will be looped automatically
  - No audio needed (it gets stripped)
  - You can point to a different path by changing 'gameplay_video' in config.json
